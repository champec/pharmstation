  - Disciplinary actions

  - Fitness to practice concerns
  - References and recruitment records

  - Correspondence with GPhC
  - Communications with healthcare bodies
  - Regulator notifications
  - Important governance decisions
  - Board or management meeting minutes

  - How long records must be kept
  - Who can access RP records
  - Secure storage requirements
  - Digital storage vs physical files
