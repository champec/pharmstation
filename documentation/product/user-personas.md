#### 1. Primary Persona: Sarah - Independent Pharmacy Owner

**Demographics**:
- Age: 45
- Role: Pharmacy Owner & Superintendent Pharmacist
- Location: Market town, England
- Experience: 20 years as pharmacist, 10 years as owner

**Goals**:
- Maintain GPhC compliance without stress
- Spend less time on paperwork, more time with patients
- Modernize the pharmacy
- Reduce risk of inspection failures

**Pain Points**:
- Handwritten registers are time-consuming
- Worries about errors in CD register
- Difficult to search old records
- Paper registers at risk of damage/loss

**Tech Savviness**: Moderate (uses email, basic computer skills)

**Behaviors**:
- Checks CD register daily
- Signs RP log 5 days/week
- Attends 1-2 conferences per year
- Active in local pharmacy network

**Quotes**:
- "I just want something that works and keeps me compliant"
- "I don't have time to learn complicated software"

**How PharmStation Helps**:
- Fast, simple CD register entry
- Automatic RP timestamping
- Export for inspections (peace of mind)
- Affordable for single pharmacy

#### 2. Secondary Persona: James - Responsible Pharmacist (Employee)

**Demographics**:
- Age: 32
- Role: Employed Responsible Pharmacist
- Location: City center, England
- Experience: 8 years as pharmacist

**Goals**:
- Do his job efficiently
- Maintain professional standards
- Avoid compliance errors that could affect registration
- Learn best practices

**Pain Points**:
- Different record-keeping systems at different pharmacies (locum work)
- Unclear what was done on previous shifts
- Handwritten logs hard to read (poor handwriting)

**Tech Savviness**: High (digital native, uses apps for everything)

**Behaviors**:
- Works 3-4 shifts per week
- Does locum work at multiple pharmacies
- Active on pharmacy social media groups
- Uses smartphone constantly

**Quotes**:
- "I want to sign in and get on with my day"
- "Why are we still using paper in 2026?"

**How PharmStation Helps**:
- One-click RP sign-in
- Clear handover notes from previous shift
- Consistent interface across pharmacies
- Mobile app for quick tasks

#### 3. Supporting Persona: Rachel - Pharmacy Technician

**Demographics**:
- Age: 28
- Role: Accredited Pharmacy Technician
- Location: Suburban pharmacy, England
- Experience: 6 years

**Goals**:
- Support pharmacist efficiently
- Learn and develop professionally
- Take on more responsibility

**Pain Points**:
- Limited access to information (only pharmacist can view some records)
- Unclear SOPs for some tasks
- Wants to help with CD register but can't (handwriting not allowed by some pharmacies)

**Tech Savviness**: High

**Behaviors**:
- Manages majority of dispensing
- Handles patient consultations (minor ailments)
- Organizes stock and date checking
- Completes fridge and cleaning logs

**Quotes**:
- "I could help more if I had the right tools"
- "I want to be part of the solution, not just take messages"

**How PharmStation Helps**:
- Appropriate permissions (can view, can assist with entry)
- Access to SOPs when needed
- Can manage compliance logs
- Handover notes visibility

#### 4. Decision Maker Persona: David - Pharmacy Chain Director

**Demographics**:
- Age: 52
- Role: Operations Director, 8-pharmacy chain
- Location: England
- Experience: 25 years, former superintendent pharmacist

**Goals**:
- Standardize operations across all locations
- Ensure compliance at all pharmacies
- Reduce administrative burden on store teams
- Centralized oversight and reporting

**Pain Points**:
- Inconsistent record-keeping across locations
- Can't easily audit all pharmacies
- Time-consuming to prepare for inspections
- Different systems at different sites

**Tech Savviness**: Moderate

**Behaviors**:
- Visits each pharmacy monthly
- Reviews incident reports
- Prepares for GPhC inspections
- Budget owner for technology investments

**Quotes**:
- "I need to know all my pharmacies are compliant"
- "Standardization is key to scaling"

**How PharmStation Helps**:
- Multi-pharmacy dashboard (Enterprise tier)
- Consistent processes across all sites
- Central reporting and audit
- Reduce training burden (same system everywhere)
