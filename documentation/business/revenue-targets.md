#### 7. Revenue Assumptions

**Conversion Rates**:
- Free to Starter: 30%
- Starter to Professional: 20%
- Professional to Enterprise (for chains): 10%

**Churn Rates**:
- Freemium: 30% annual (high churn acceptable)
- Starter: 15% annual
- Professional: 10% annual
- Enterprise: 5% annual

**Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**:
- Starter: £29/mo × 12 months / 0.15 = £2,320
- Professional: £79/mo × 12 months / 0.10 = £9,480
- Enterprise: £199/mo × 12 months / 0.05 = £47,760

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**:
- Target: £500 per paying customer
- Goal LTV:CAC ratio: >3:1
