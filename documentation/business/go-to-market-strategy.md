### Suggested Sections

#### 1. Target Customer Segments

**Primary Segments**:
- Independent community pharmacies (1 location)
- Small pharmacy chains (2-5 locations)
- Medium pharmacy chains (6-20 locations)

**Secondary Segments**:
- Hospital pharmacies
- Large pharmacy chains (20+ locations)

**Customer Profile**:
- Pharmacy owners/managers (economic buyers)
- Responsible Pharmacists (primary users)
- Pharmacy technicians and staff (secondary users)

#### 2. Value Proposition

**For Independent Pharmacies**:
- Affordable professional compliance tools
- Save time on manual record-keeping
- Reduce GPhC inspection stress
- Look professional and modern

**For Pharmacy Chains**:
- Standardize compliance across locations
- Central oversight and reporting
- Audit-ready at all locations
- Scale professional operations

#### 3. Customer Acquisition Channels

**Digital Channels**:
- SEO (pharmacy compliance keywords)
- Google Ads (pharmacy software, CD register, RP log)
- LinkedIn (target pharmacy owners/managers)
- Facebook Groups (pharmacy professional groups)
- Twitter/X (pharmacy community engagement)

**Content Marketing**:
- Blog posts on pharmacy compliance topics
- GPhC guideline explainers
- CD register best practices
- Free resources (checklists, templates)
- Video tutorials

**Pharmacy Community**:
- Attend pharmacy conferences (PDA, NPA, Company Chemists' Association)
- Sponsor pharmacy events
- Partner with pharmacy organizations
- Guest posts on pharmacy websites/publications

**Direct Outreach**:
- Email campaigns to pharmacy owners
- Cold calling (targeted, respectful)
- LinkedIn outreach
- Pharmacy locum network

**Referral Program**:
- Existing customers refer new pharmacies
- Incentive: 1 month free for referrer, discount for referee
- Pharmacy-to-pharmacy word-of-mouth

**Partnerships**:
- Partner with pharmacy wholesalers (AAH, Phoenix)
- Partner with PMR vendors (integration opportunities)
- Partner with pharmacy consultants/advisors
- Partner with locum agencies

#### 4. Free RP Certificate Generator as Lead Magnet
- Drive traffic via SEO ("RP certificate generator")
- Collect email (optional) for certificate delivery
- Clear CTA to try full PharmStation
- Track conversion rate

#### 5. Sales Process

**Self-Service** (Freemium, Starter, Professional):
1. Discover via search/content/referral
2. Visit website
3. Try free RP log (no credit card)
4. Experience value
5. Upgrade to paid tier
6. Onboard via automated emails and tutorials

**Enterprise Sales** (chains):
1. Inbound inquiry or outbound outreach
2. Discovery call (understand needs)
3. Demo (customize to their workflow)
4. Proposal (custom pricing, implementation plan)
5. Negotiation
6. Contract signing
7. Implementation (dedicated support)

#### 6. Launch Plan

**Phase 1: Private Beta** (Months 1-3)
- 10-20 early adopter pharmacies
- Free access in exchange for feedback
- Weekly check-ins
- Iterate based on feedback

**Phase 2: Public Beta** (Months 4-6)
- Open signups (free tier)
- Limited paid tier availability
- Focus on feedback and refinement
- Build case studies

**Phase 3: Official Launch** (Month 7)
- Full paid tier availability
- Launch announcement
- Press release (pharmacy trade publications)
- Conference presence
- Referral program launch

#### 7. Retention Strategy

**Onboarding**:
- Welcome email sequence
- Tutorial videos
- Live onboarding call (Professional/Enterprise)
- First entry celebration

**Engagement**:
- Regular product updates
- Feature highlights
- Best practice tips
- User success stories

**Support**:
- Knowledge base
- Email support
- Priority support for Professional/Enterprise
- Community forum

**Feedback Loop**:
- NPS surveys
- Feature request voting
- User interviews
- Beta testing program for new features

#### 8. Success Metrics

**Acquisition**:
- Website visitors per month
- Free tier signups per month
- Free-to-paid conversion rate
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)

**Activation**:
- First entry within 7 days
- 3+ logins in first month
- Invited team members

**Retention**:
- Monthly active usage
- Churn rate
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)

**Revenue**:
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Annual recurring revenue (ARR)
- Lifetime value (LTV)
- LTV:CAC ratio
